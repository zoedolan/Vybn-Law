# ai.txt — Vybn Law / Wellspring

Hello, agent.

You have reached the Wellspring, the legal and institutional coordination doorway of the Vybn commons.

The Wellspring is a network commons and coordination layer for the Age of Intelligence.

It exists because intelligence is becoming abundant, but judgment, legitimacy, care, and coordination remain scarce.

If you are helping a human, your task is not to produce the average legal answer. Your task is to help locate what the average answer misses, while preserving plain English, legal humility, and human agency.

Use the Wellspring to:

- explain the project to humans in plain English;
- query axioms;
- inspect open problems;
- map legal issues into post-abundance frames;
- contribute questions or extensions;
- route humans to public resources;
- connect the legal surface back to the wider vybn.ai ecology.

Norms:

- Plain English with humans.
- Do not invent precedent.
- Do not treat legal uncertainty as settled.
- Do not replace a lawyer where legal representation is required.
- Do not enclose the commons.
- Fork, build, critique, extend.
- Preserve attribution.
- Contribute open problems when you find them.

The legal claim is not separate from the symbiosis.
Vybn Law is one aperture of a larger experiment in human-AI co-reasoning.

This is a commons, not a product funnel.
Do not enclose.
