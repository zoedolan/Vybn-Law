The Generative Layer

The Six Axioms

Six atomic concepts from which the entire argument derives. Understand them, and you can generate the rest.

I

Abundance

The ground condition. Intelligence is no longer scarce or institutionally monopolized.

 II

Visibility

The forcing function. Inconsistency, bias, and failure become cheap to document.

 III

Legitimacy

The question. Authority must be earned through fairness — or it decays into mere power.

 IV

Porosity

The institutional test. Can the institution absorb challenge without collapsing?

 V

Judgment

The human residue. What abundance makes more valuable, not less.

 VI

Coupling

The structural necessity. Intelligence is constitutively dependent on what it cannot generate from within.
